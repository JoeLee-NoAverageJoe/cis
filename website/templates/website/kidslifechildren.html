Kid's Life Children's Ministries

We in Kid’s Life believe that the lives of children are impacted most effectively by a holistic approach. A holistic approach impacts lives mentally, physically, spiritually and socially.

  • Mentally through education to children’s minds.
  • Physically through compassion to have children’s basic needs met.
  • Spiritually through evangelism to reach children for Christ.
  • Socially through discipleship to strengthen children’s faith.

Sunday School
Children from newborn thru 6th grade participate in age appropriate small group classes each Sunday morning at 9:30 a.m.

Children's Church
Childrens Church implements fun and adventure in our Children’s Church time each week for children in Kindergarten thru 4th grade on Sunday mornings. Children participate in an age appropriate worship service.

Bible Bucks & The Gospel Store
Bible Bucks are earned by kids who participate in the AWANA program. Bible Bucks are awarded for attendance, Bible memorization, bringing a friend, etc. Once a month, Bible Bucks may be traded for prizes at the Gospel Store.

AWANA
AWANA is the Wednesday kids ministry in which children from preschool thru 6th grade focus on spiritual formation, ministry, Nazarene heritage and beliefs, and scouting. Benson Buddies is a special program for children ages Toddler through Kindergarten. Wednesdays at 6:30 p.m.

Kidz Biz
Kidz Biz is the summer program that includes great music, games, lesson time and special days for children Toddler thru 6th grade. Kidz Biz meets on Wednesday evenings at 7:00 p.m. during the summer months.

Vacation Bible School
Vacation Bible School is held each summer for children Preschool through 6th grade.
